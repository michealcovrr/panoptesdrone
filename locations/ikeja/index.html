Ikeja: Lagos Mainland's Commercial Hub

As the capital of Lagos State and home to the Lagos State Government Secretariat in Alausa, Ikeja is the administrative and commercial engine of the mainland. The area's mix of government infrastructure, established residential estates (Ikeja GRA), and bustling commercial corridors creates diverse demand for drone services ‚Äî from construction site monitoring to corporate video production.

Ikeja is also a major hub for the Nigerian media industry.
