FAQ

Drone Rental Questions Answered

Do I need a drone licence to rent?

For recreational and personal use, no formal NCAA licence is required in Nigeria. However, for commercial drone operations (real estate marketing, advertising, events for pay), you are legally required to use a licensed operator. Our "With Operator" package covers this automatically — your shoot is fully compliant.

What happens if the drone is damaged during my rental?

Minor wear and tear is covered under normal use. Damage caused by pilot error, crash, or negligence is charged against the security deposit. We assess all returns transparently before deposit release. We strongly recommend booking the operator option if you have any uncertainty about your flying ability.

Can I fly anywhere in Lagos with a rented drone?

No — Lagos has airspace restrictions, particularly around Murtala Muhammed Airport, Victoria Island, Ikoyi, and certain government zones. We check your planned flight location before rental and advise on any restrictions. Some areas require advance notice to the NCAA. We handle this guidance as part of the briefing.

Can I extend my rental on the day?

Extensions are subject to availability and must be agreed before the original rental window closes. We cannot guarantee extensions if another booking follows yours. Contact us during your rental period if you anticipate needing more time.

What if the weather is bad on my rental day?

Heavy rain, storms, and high winds make drone flight unsafe and are outside our equipment's operating parameters. We monitor weather with you and can reschedule your rental at no penalty if conditions are genuinely unsafe on your booked date. Light overcast is usually fine — many of the best drone shots happen on cloudy days.

Do you deliver the drone to my location?

Standard pickup and return is at our Lagos base. For shoots in remote areas or where distance makes pickup impractical, we can arrange on-location delivery for an additional logistics fee. Discuss your location with us when booking.

Is editing included with self-fly rental?

Self-fly rentals give you raw footage on your memory cards — editing is your responsibility. If you want edited deliverables, book the operator package which includes professional post-production. We can also quote separately for editing your self-flown raw footage.
