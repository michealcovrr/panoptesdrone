Lekki's Construction Boom Needs Aerial Oversight

Lekki is Lagos's most active construction zone. The Lekki-Epe corridor, Free Trade Zone, and Phase 1 tower projects are reshaping the peninsula. Aerial monitoring gives developers, project managers, and investors real-time visibility into site progress without scaffolding or interrupting work crews.

Construction monitoring in Lekki covers:
  • Lekki Free Trade Zone — Document industrial-scale infrastructure development with periodic flyovers.
  • Phase 1 High-Rises — Track vertical progress on residential towers with consistent comparison shots.
  • Chevron Drive Estates — Monitor gated community builds from foundation to finishing.
  • Lekki-Epe Expressway Projects — Road infrastructure and commercial developments along the corridor.
  • Ajah New Estates — Document phased development across multiple plots for investor updates.
