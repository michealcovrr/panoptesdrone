Why It Matters

Properties with Aerial Media Sell Faster

Give Buyers the Full Picture

Ground-level photos tell half the story. Aerial footage reveals the complete layout — the estate boundaries, surrounding infrastructure, proximity to water, neighboring amenities, and contextual position within the neighborhood. Buyers who see the full picture make faster, more confident decisions.

What aerial media shows that ground photos can't:
  • Estate Layouts — Show the full compound, gates, landscaping, and property footprint from above.
  • Location Context — Proximity to Lekki-Epe Expressway, the lagoon, or key landmarks — visible from the air.
  • Rooftop Features — Rooftop pools, penthouses, and terraces that can't be captured from the ground.
  • Neighborhood Premium — Ikoyi, Banana Island, and VI properties look dramatically different — and more valuable — from above.
  • Development Scale — For off-plan or new estate launches, aerial footage communicates scale and ambition instantly.
