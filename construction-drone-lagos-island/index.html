Lagos Island's Renewal Needs Visual Progress

Lagos Island is seeing significant infrastructure investment — bridge approaches, marina redevelopment, and commercial building projects that are reshaping the original city centre. Aerial monitoring documents this transformation for developers, government agencies, and investors who need consistent visual progress reports.

Construction documentation on Lagos Island:
  • Marina Redevelopment — Track waterfront infrastructure and building projects along the Marina.
  • Bridge Infrastructure — Document works on Eko Bridge and Carter Bridge approaches.
  • Broad Street Commercial Projects — Monitor office building renovations and new builds.
  • CMS Area Developments — Track mixed-use and institutional construction projects.
  • Market Area Infrastructure — Document infrastructure improvements around Balogun and Idumota.
